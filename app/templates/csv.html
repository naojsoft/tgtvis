CSV Help
- Upload csv with/out header file(s). Checkbox for header
- Csv with header, header required keywords are "name", "ra", "dec", and "equinox" in any order. lowercae/mixed-case/upercase are acceptable.
- Csv without header, "name", "ra", "dec", and "equinox" have to be in order.
- Ra/Dec format: hhmmss.s*/hh:mm:ss.s*, +-dd:mm:ss.s*/ddmmss.s* or degree. Checkbox for either hms/dms or degree
e.g.
csv with header.  ra/dec in hms/dms
ra, Dec, equinox, NAME,  comment,  etc  
000000.00, +000000.0, 2000.0, AAA,  foo, 123
00:00:00.00, +00:00:00.0, 2000.0, BBB, hoo, abc 

csv with header.  ra/dec in deg
ra, Dec, equinox, NAME
0.00, 0.0, 2000.0, subaru

csv without header.  ra/dec in hms/dms
HI, 000000.00, +000000.0, 2000.0
HI, 00:50:00.00, -10:00:00.0, 2000.0

csv without header.  ra/dec in degree
HI, 359.99, 89.99, 2000.0
